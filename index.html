Nigeria

The Federal Republic of Nigeria is a large and diverse country located in West Africa. Often called the "Giant of Africa," it's the continent's most populous nation, with an estimated population of over 230 million people. Nigeria is a federation of 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory, with its capital city, Abuja, situated in the center of the country.Nigeria has a varied landscape, with its geography and climate changing from south to north. The southern region has a tropical rainforest climate, characterized by high rainfall and humidity. As you move northward, the climate transitions to a tropical savanna with distinct wet and dry seasons. The far north has a semi-arid Sahel climate, which is hot and dry with a short rainy season.
